05 — Website & Brand Overhaul

The current site
undersells everything.

This isn't a cosmetic refresh — it's a repositioning to match what you actually deliver.

What's wrong right now
  • Rotating hero with generic stock photography — zero connection to Hawaii or Fernando
  • Typography reads as generic — somewhere between a dental office and a craft beer brand
  • No real logo — just the business name in a web font
  • Nothing signals Honolulu or the warmth of a locally-rooted business
  • Two Yahoo email addresses visible to the public
  • Clunky nav as a second tier — wastes space, feels dated
What the new site needs
  • A proper logo — wordmark that works across web, print, and social
  • A refined color palette — teal elevated with warm neutrals that signal luxury, not clinical
  • Real photography: treatment close-ups, Hawaiian lifestyle imagery — Fernando working, not a stock model
  • One clean nav row — prominent Book Now, no second bar
  • Professional email — hello@hydraheaven.com
  • Dedicated Fernando bio — 20 years of experience and Lomi Lomi specialization are selling points, not footnotes

Lead with the combo packages. "Honolulu's only HydraFacial + Lomi Lomi massage studio" is a more powerful opening line than "3 Steps, 30 Minutes." That's your moat — put it front and center.

"You're not going to a salon. You're getting personal, expert attention from a specialist in a private studio. That's a luxury signal when framed correctly."
